  • IM Plan for COVID-19

    The IM plan aims to layout the fundamentals of Information Management (IM) for the COVID-19 outbreak Emergency Operation (one pager in this link). Although regional and global strategies exist, due to unprecedented conditions such as new work dynamics, remoteness and pervasive impact to all society, there is an urgent need to detail how the IM component in the IFRC and National Societies will adapt those strategies to the COVID-19 context.

    The interactive presentation shows a detailed analysis of the first 4 months of the IM work withing the operation of COVID-19. It also includes a breakdown of the goals laid out by the plan.
